Why Should I Buy???

Farmers can't sell their produce at their own price because all commodities prices are fixed or govern by market. Why Pay the Middlemen? We firmly believe that everyone gets a 'Fair' price when commodities are transferred directly between suppliers and consumers. Our vision is to build a place where customers can buy goods directly from farmers/producers. Straight from farmers emerged as there were not many ecommerce options focusing on the core farming and the small businesses community. We commit to providing a generous selection of goods, low prices, secure online shopping and a world class ecommerce multi-vendor marketplace for buyers and sellers. Additionally, we serve as a platform to sell your home grown produce. We are on a mission to make Straight from Farmers a global online marketplace for customers and sellers to have an easy, hassle-free, secure and trustworthy trade.
